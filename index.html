Frequently Asked Questions

Game Issues

Q: The app is crashing or not responding
A: Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device and ensure you have the latest app version installed.

Q: I lost my game progress
A: Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you've deleted the app or switched devices, unfortunately the progress cannot be recovered as we don't store data in the cloud.

Q: How do I use notes/candidates?
A: Tap the pencil icon in the bottom controls to toggle notes mode. In notes mode, tapping numbers will add small candidate numbers to cells instead of filling them.

Settings & Features

Q: How do I change the theme?
A: Go to Settings > Appearance > Theme. You can choose between Light, Dark, or Auto (follows system theme).

Q: Can I turn off vibrations?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Haptics and toggle "Haptic Feedback" off.

Q: What are Developer Options?
A: These are advanced features for experienced users. They can be unlocked through a secret sequence in the About section.

Technical Requirements

  • iOS: Requires iOS 13.0 or later
  • Android: Requires Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later
  • Storage: Approximately 50MB of free space
  • Internet: Not required for gameplay (offline app)

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Restart the app if you experience any issues
  • Free up device storage if the app is running slowly
  • Check that your device meets the minimum requirements
  • Try turning off other apps to free up memory
